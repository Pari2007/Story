**Story**
Maya, a girl aged 13, passionate about being a leader in the future has a lot of challenges to overcome. She had a unique perspective of being a leader. With her interest in coding she wanted to create a powerful application. She had an idea of creating a application for social good helping her attain success as well as helping the society grow!
She finally started working on an aplication that helps people be aware of the various cyber attacks going on in their surroundings and how dangerous they are.
